About F*CK Lactose

Stephane D. Groulx and Julia Ricketti are a husband and wife team who are passionate about tons of shit. Food being one of them.

Between them, they have three dietary disorders:

  • Lactose Intolerance

  • Irritable Bowel Syndrome

  • Selective Eating Disorder

Essentially, life can be hell for the Ricketti-Groulx household. Especially for the poor toilet. #ToiletAwareness

F*ck Lactose is their generous gift to all their brothers and sisters who suffer in solidarity from dietary restrictions and the oppression caused by a society that does not cater to their needs.
